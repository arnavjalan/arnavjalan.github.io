The Future of IIoT Predictive Maintenance

The Future of IIoT Predictive Maintenance research study was designed to identify the gaps between the high-level strategic and business drivers of change and the reality of implementation. For this purpose, 103 Maintenance and Reliability professionals responsible for Predictive Maintenance in their organizations across Europe, North America, and Asia Pacific were surveyed and interviewed. In addition, feedback was solicited in public forums in Asset Maintenance LinkedIn groups.
